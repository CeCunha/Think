HOW TO PLAY
  • THINK! and guess the word in 5 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid 6-letter word.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Exemple:

M is in the word and in the correct spot.

M is in the word but in the wrong spot.

N is not in the word in any spot.
